Technical FAQ

What makes Diamond Motorsport Garage different from standard garages?
We treat engineering as an art form. Our garage combines structural mechanic repairs, cashless insurance claim facilities, and high-tech detailing (Ceramic/PPF coatings) under a single, track-optimized roof with racing-grade infrastructure.
What is the expected timeline for a premium PPF installation?
A high-tier self-healing Paint Protection Film (PPF) application requires roughly 3 to 4 business days. This guarantees thorough deep paint correction, precision decontamination preparation, edge wrapping, and final infrared heat curation.
Do you offer claims across standard insurance providers?
Yes, we support straightforward claim allocations with leading insurance suppliers. Our backend team coordinates the structural evaluation processing directly to ensure your asset experiences minimal vehicle downtime.
